TORQUE TITANS

About TT

Torque titans is the official motorsports team of Coimbatore Institute of Technology. We design and manufacture quadbike (ATV) for participating in the FMAE event. Our aim is to convert the concepts learnt in the classroom into engineering wonder in the form of vehicle.


Our team has acquired time to time insight on the art of building a quad bike that earns trust and sets an everlasting trend. We unite as a team to design, fabricate and aestheticize a beast that visualizes the pride and the untiring effort of the team. Our success is the fruit of our attitude towards the challenges. We focus on our team spirit and every member's perceptions to sculpt a strategy and roar towards success

About FMAE


The Fraternity of Mechanical and Automotive engineers(FMAE) is a professional organization which organizes the quadbike design challenge event since 2016. QBDC is the India’s No 1 Quad bike design challenge and Racing Event. More than 60+ colleges participate in the QBDC Event.

Legacy of TT


CIT motorsports team was started in the year 2013. Over the past 10 years our college motorsports team has participated in various events across India and won various awards across different categories
