Mechnotron 2K25

The National Level Technical Symposium

The Department of Mechanical Engineering at CIT conducted its annual national-level technical symposium MECHNOTRON 2K25 on 21st March 2025 under the banner of the Mechanical Engineering Association (MEA). The event was envisioned to bring together young minds from various engineering institutions across the country to compete, collaborate, and contribute towards innovation in mechanical and allied engineering domains. The symposium comprised a total of 21 technical and non-technical events, meticulously planned and executed to test the creativity, critical thinking, and technical expertise of participants.
